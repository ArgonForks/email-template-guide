E-mail Template Guide

Let's make e-mail development faster.

What is it?

At the Voorhoede we like to get things automated, not only because it saves you lots of time but also because its fun! This e-mail template guide is based on our front-end guide. And generates html e-mails for you. It automatically inserts your styles inline but keeps your mediaqueries intact. It is also configured to work with Litmus to test your e-mails on multiple clients with a single command!

Do you have any questions about the guide itself or the project? Take a look at our project wiki.

Some tasks it takes out of hand:

  • Simply adding and removing a modules.
  • Automatically inlining CSS styles.
  • Extracting all mediaqueries used and inserting them into the HEAD of your template. Responsive templates, HOORAY!!
  • Testing your e-mail layouts with Litmus.
  • Deliver a self-contained package that can be sent or hosted easily.

Demo template

We have created a simple demo template for you, just as a start. You can use the header bar to navigate to the template. Or view the components it's made out of.
