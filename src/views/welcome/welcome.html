Why did we create this guide?

At the Voorhoede we like to get things automated, not only because it saves you a lot of time but we also because its fun! This email-guide is based on our front-end guide.

What can you expect?

The purpose of this project is to roll out mailings rather painless and fast. If you have any questions you can take a look at our project wiki.

  • Adding and removing a modules
  • Automatically inlining CSS styles
  • Testing your newsletter layouts with Litmus
  • Deliver a self-contained packaged that can be sent or hosted easily
