Email Template Guide

Let's make email development faster.

What is it?

At the Voorhoede we like to get things automated, not only because it saves you a lot of time but we also because its fun! This email-guide is based on our front-end guide. And generates html emails for you. It automattically inserts your styles inline but keeps your mediaqueries. It is also configurated to work with Litmus to test your emails with a single command!

Do you have any questions about the guide itself or the project? Take a look at our project wiki

Some tasks it takes out of hand:

  • Simply adding and removing a modules
  • Automatically inlining CSS styles
  • Testing your newsletter layouts with Litmus
  • Deliver a self-contained packaged that can be sent or hosted easily

Demo template

We have created a simple demo template for you, just as a start. You can use the header bar to navigate to the template.
